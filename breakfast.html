Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 100g flour
  • 3 eggs flour
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g butter milk
  • Variety of berries
  • Chocolate syrup
  • 1tsp salt

Procedure

  1. Beat the eggs and then mix flour, milk and salt into it.
  2. Heat up the pan with some butter
  3. Use a big laddle and spread a scoop of it on the pan.
  4. Fry 2 minutes on each sides.
  5. Add fresh berries and chocolate syrup on them before serving.
